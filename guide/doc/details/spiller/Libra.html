Leo

Traits to Focus On

  • Cooperation
  • Being diplomatic and courteous
  • Being more in tune with other people's needs
  • Unselfishness: helping without expecting something in return
  • Creating win/win situations
  • Sharing
  • Seeing a situation through someone else's eyes
  • Expressing his/her identity

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Acting without forethought
  • Rushing in, putting self forward before thinking about the situation first
  • Not realizing other people's need for support
  • Self centeredness
  • Selfishness
  • Not being good with money
  • Expecting others to be like you
  • Not caring how you look in others' eyes
  • Stubbornly refusing to meet the other side midway
  • Fits of anger
  • Being excessively worried about on life

The Achilles Heel for this person is selfishness. Interestingly enough, these people will never find an environment where their needs are constantly met first, instead, they have to find a partner to whom they can give. They will be satisfied if they are with people who can see who they are, appreciate them and respond them in return. The biggest trap for them is an endless quest for independence, but the life, the situation they are given is not organized to let this happen. These people will find happiness when they are supporting someone in such a degree that they are losing themselves in the relationship. Ironically once they do that, they realize their true self being and become happier.

These people spent many previous lifetimes as warriors, they could only depend on themselves, they were on the battlefield. Now things are different.
