Cancer

Traits to Focus On

  • Being aware of your feelings and acknowledging them
  • Emphaty
  • Supporting another person, either physically, emotionally
  • Openly discussing your feelings and shortcomings
  • Modesty
  • Accepting other people's shortcomings and changing moods without being judgemental
  • Being centered on your feelings, emotional being

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Wanting to control anything and everything
  • A tendency to rush in to control, manage something before understanding all the issues that are involved
  • Obsessing too much about the goal, losing sight of the process
  • Feeling that you are responsible for everything
  • In close relationships hiding your fears, shortcomings
  • Doing things to gain respect or awe of someone else
  • Obsessing what other people think while forgetting to focus on your own emotions
  • Following an accepted norm instead of what doing is right
  • Thinking that in order for a task, goal to be important, it must be hard to do, to achieve

The Achilles Heel of this person is a constant need to control. "If I can fix someone / everyone else's life, then I can focus on myself". But he will never be able to control enough things in a degree that will make him feel secure. When this person is taking responsibility for others' lives, he jeapordizes someone else's sense of responsibility. Ex-Manager people must stay away from seeking approval for their contribution, they must provide constructive support and internally acknowledge their support's importance.
