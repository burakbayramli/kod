Sagittarius

Traits to Focus On

  • Trusting gut feeling, ability of foreseeing events, and hidden hand that guides
  • Talking from / using higher, collective knowledge, wisdom
  • Being spontaneous, developing a sense of adventure, freedom
  • Communicating without sensoring self
  • Trusting self
  • Spending time alone in nature
  • Patience
  • While listening, also listening to the meaning behind the words

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Being and remaining undecided
  • Overanalyzing and second guessing your conclusion on what someone said long after they said it
  • Constantly looking for more information
  • Saying things that others want to hear
  • Invalidating gut feeling with logic
  • Gossip
  • Being impatient, wanting answers really fast
  • Including perception of self, trusting on others' perceptions more than you trust your own

The Achilles Heel of whose these people is their need for mental security. "If I can understand what other people think and say the right thing that will make them to agree with me, I will always feel happy". This can lead them to a trap of unending quest for more information, and is unfruitful, because they can never understand other people thoughts to a degree that will help him say the right thing. They need to let go of control and trust their instincts, to pay attention to their own reality. When they do that, this will bring forth their honesty, which in turn, help them find the happiness and security they need.
