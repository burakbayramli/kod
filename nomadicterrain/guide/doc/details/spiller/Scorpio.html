Scorpio

Traits to Focus On

  • Self discipline
  • Choosing constructive change
  • Letting go of things that bring low energy and inactiveness
  • Letting go of unnecessary possessions.
  • Getting enjoyment from things without having to own them
  • Accepting support from others (money, ideas, opportunities)
  • Enjoying high-risk situations that make one feel alive
  • Being aware of other people's psychology (their needs, wants, and motives)
  • Being open and ready to partner with people, be ready to join forces

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Being too lax, too comfortable and being attached to status quo
  • Being a pack rat
  • Excessive ownership
  • Constantly rehashing decisions made in the past
  • Stubbornnes
  • Getting too attached to bodily needs, pleasures
  • Even though another way is easier, insisting on doing things using the older (and harder) way.
  • Stubbornly refusing change and other people's ideas

Achilles Heel for Comfort Zone people is, well, their comfort, their lax attitude to life. Their ultimate goal in life is to be relaxed and own things. But this person will never own enough things to make them feel "relaxed". Their need to be in high energy situation and must let their lax attitude go. They need to focus on partnering with others instead of focusing too much on themselves, their comfort. Irony is when they partner with someone else, this relationship can make them rich.
