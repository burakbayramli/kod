Aquarius

Traits to Focus On

  • Being objective (seeing the big picture)
  • Developing relationships, wanting friendship
  • Making decisions that are optimal for the group
  • Being open to ideas that are outside the mainstream
  • Fighting for humanitarian issues
  • Active participation in groups
  • Being aware of equality of men
  • To form relationships with people other than through their various roles in life - gardeners, doctors, lovers, etc.
  • Creating win/win situations
  • Noticing that everyone unique and special

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Insisting on getting what you want
  • Making changes simply in order to exercise authority
  • An obsession with risk taking, both in love and gambling
  • Stubborness, being headstrong
  • Constant seeking of approval
  • Melodramatic tendencies
  • Doing what others expect of you instead of what is in your heart
  • Being excessively passionate, or, being excessive in general
  • Not being aware of others' importance
  • Arrogant responses which are rooted in fear

Achilles Heel of these people is a constant seeking of approval. They must develop an ability of self-approval and must stick to their ideas which are out of the mainstream, not requiring approval from others.
