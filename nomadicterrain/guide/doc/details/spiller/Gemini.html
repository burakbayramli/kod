Gemini

Traits to Focus On

  • Healty curiosity
  • Asking people questions in order to understand what they think
  • Seeing two sides of a situation
  • Being measured, gentleness
  • Logic
  • Expressing internal divisions, paradoxes
  • Having a positive approach to life and other people
  • Entertaining others, trying to make them happy
  • Putting ideas forward in a non-threathening manner
  • Listening
  • Being open to new ideas and experiences
  • Seeking knowledge rooted in concepts before making a decision on something

Traits to Leave Behind

  • Being sure that you are right
  • Being cold to others, staying at a distance
  • Assuming that everyone else knows where they stand
  • Assuming to know what someone else thinks before listening to what they are saying
  • A need for being right all the time
  • Being on the side of "reality" disregarding what other people think
  • Taking shortcuts
  • Taking self to seriously, having a boring approach to life
  • Acting on gut feeling without checking concepts, knowledge
  • Being adamantly against ideas which are foreign to your system of thought
  • Deciding on current events too quickly simply based on what happened in the past

The Achilles Heel of such people is their obsessiveness with being always right. This tendency might push them toward a unending quest of seeking "The Truth", but this is an unfruitful undertaking. Since it is impossible to be right all the time, this person never feels happiness. If, however, they become modest and humble enough to be open to other people, to their outlook and their ideas which might not match to their experience, they can get to know people better and other's like him for it. This person needs to drop his quest for "absolute Truth", but instead form relationships with people as they are, start learning from them and listening to them.
