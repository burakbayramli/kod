Life Purpose

Integrity and Wisdom

In the Positive

Lives in alignment with higher principles; leads by example; manifests depth, charisma, and wisdom.

In the Negative

Has lost sight of higher purpose; feels lonely and cutoff; locked in the mind; out of touch with heart and intuitive guidance; fanatical and hypocritical.
