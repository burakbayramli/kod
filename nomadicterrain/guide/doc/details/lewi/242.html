Almost everyone born in the last decade of 20th centurycarries this Uranus-Neptune conjunct. Uranus is clarity, it's volatile, carries a power of social responsibility. Uranus is out of the ordinariness, it is oddness, it is radicalness and progressiveness. It is unordinarily open.

Neptune, on the other hand, is hard to describe. Abstract. It is poetic vision, a dense presence of emotion. It is secretive, takes a while to prepare. What strong characteristic traits Uranus makes open, Neptune hides and combines. Uranus is calm, Neptune mixes that up.

The possessor of this angle might have to wrestle between clarity and complexity; Too much clarity makes life too unmystical and bland, too much complexity would make it impossible to manage, the possessor will have to strike a good balance between these two extremes.
